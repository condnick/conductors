Paragraphs

The training of performing musicians rests on the twin pillars of music and technique. Effective teaching nurtures innate musicianship and provides a technical framework with which to transmit this musicianship. However, unique amongst performing musicians, the conductor relies on others to produce sound. In addition to musicianship and technique, a conductor requires a practical and theoretical understanding of leadership in order to realize their potential as inspirational musicians.
