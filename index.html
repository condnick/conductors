Academic research
Nicholas Logie has recently completed a Ph.D at the Open University researching into the role of leadership in conducting orchestras. Unique amongst performing musicians, the conductor relies on others to produce sound. In addition to musicianship and technique, a conductor requires a practical and theoretical understanding of leadership in order to realise his or her potential as an inspirational musician.
